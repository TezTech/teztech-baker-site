Instant Payouts

We payout as soon as your delegation has registered to us. If you delegate to us during cycle 50, we will receive your delegation rights at the start of cycle 52 or 53, and this is when we will pay you. That means you will get paid within 3-9 days after delegating to us. This is 11 cycles faster than most other baking services.

Guaranteed Rewards

You will receive rewards, even if we miss a block or endorsement, that's our guarantee. We will take full responsiblity for any missed blocks and losses due to double baking. Delegations that push us passed our capacity will not receive rewards, ensuring that your rewards are not diluted.

Annonymity

No signups or registration required, all rewards will be delivered to your delegator address - no questions asked. You can view and monitor our account from our Dashboard, and optionally signup for email notifications when rewards are delivered (coming soon).

Infrastructure

We are one of the most experience teams when it comes to undertstanding the Tezos protocol, running/maintaining nodes, and baking on Tezos. We have operated baking nodes as early as baking itself and have a vast amount of knowledge surrounding it.

We will run a network of edge nodes, with high availability and connectivity. This network will be distrubted around the world, and will ensure an up to date mempool. We operate a similar network that is used to power the TezBox wallet.

Our baking machines will be run in private, connected via a VPN. Our baking keys will be secured on a hardware wallet. Multiple redundancies will be in place to ensure 100% uptime.

On top of this, we wil operate different monitoring services and DDOS protection to keep or baking operation running smoothly and without issues.
